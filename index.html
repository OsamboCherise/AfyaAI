How to Use Afya AI Chatbot

  • Start a conversation by typing in the message box or using the quick replies
  • Share your feelings - you can describe your current emotional state
  • Ask for help with specific issues like anxiety, stress, or sadness
  • Request techniques for mindfulness, breathing, or coping strategies
  • Be specific about what you need help with for more tailored responses
  • Try the quick reply buttons to get started quickly with common topics
  • Remember I'm an AI assistant, not a replacement for professional help in crisis situations
